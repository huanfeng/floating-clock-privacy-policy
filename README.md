# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: December 17, 2025**

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to **[Floating Clock]** ("the App"). This App is developed and operated by **[huanfeng]** ("we," "us," or "our").

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains that **we do not collect, store, or share any of your personal information.** The App is designed to function 100% offline.

## 2. Data Collection and Use

### 2.1 No Personal Data Collected
We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information, including but not limited to your name, phone number, email address, location data, device ID, or IP address.

### 2.2 Local Storage
Any settings or configurations (such as timer presets or visual themes) are stored locally on your device using Android's standard shared preferences or local database. This data never leaves your device.

## 3. Permissions

To provide its core functionality, the App requires the following specific permission. We want to be transparent about why we need it:

* **Display over other apps (SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW)**
    * **Purpose:** This permission is essential for the App's core feature: displaying a floating clock or timer overlay on top of other applications.
    * **Privacy Statement:** This permission is used **strictly for rendering the floating UI**. We do not use this permission to capture screen content, record user input, or monitor your activities in other apps.

## 4. Third-Party Services

As a purely offline application, we do not integrate any third-party SDKs for advertising, analytics, or user tracking.

*Note: The app distribution platform (Google Play Store) may collect anonymous diagnostic data (e.g., crash logs or installation metrics) subject to Google's own privacy policies.*

## 5. Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13. As the App functions offline and does not collect data, it is safe for users of all ages.

## 6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

## 7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at:

* **Email:** [fengsoft1@gmail.com]

**生效日期：2025年12月17日**

## 1. 引言

欢迎使用 **[悬浮时钟]**（以下简称“本应用”）。本应用由 **[huanfeng]**（以下简称“我们”）开发。

我们非常重视您的隐私。本隐私权政策旨在向您说明，本应用作为一个**纯离线工具**，我们如何处理您的信息。

**简而言之：我们不收集、不存储、也不向第三方传输您的任何个人身份信息。**

## 2. 数据收集与使用

### 2.1 个人信息
本应用完全离线运行。我们**不会**收集您的姓名、电话号码、电子邮件地址、位置信息或任何其他个人身份信息。

### 2.2 用户生成内容
本应用中保存的所有设置（如倒计时设置、时钟样式配置）均仅存储在您的设备本地。这些数据不会上传到任何服务器。

## 3. 权限使用说明

为了实现核心功能，本应用需要申请以下敏感权限，我们在此对权限用途进行明确说明：

* **显示在其他应用的上层 (Display over other apps / SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW)**：
    * **用途**：本应用是一个悬浮时钟工具。此权限用于在您使用其他应用时，能够在屏幕最顶层显示时间或倒计时悬浮窗。
    * **隐私说明**：此权限仅用于渲染 UI 界面，**绝对不会**用于监控您的屏幕内容、截屏或收集您在其他应用中的操作记录。

由于本应用是纯离线应用，我们没有集成用于广告追踪或数据分析的第三方 SDK。

*注意：应用的分发平台（Google Play Store）可能会收集一些关于安装和崩溃的匿名诊断数据，这受 Google Play 服务条款的管辖。*

## 5. 儿童隐私

本应用不针对 13 岁以下的儿童，且不包含任何收集儿童信息的功能。如果您是家长或监护人，并且发现我们在不知情的情况下收集了您孩子的信息（虽然本应用不具备联网收集功能），请联系我们，我们将立即删除相关本地数据处理逻辑。

我们可能会随时更新我们的隐私权政策。因此，建议您定期查看本页面以了解是否有任何更改。我们会通过在此页面上发布新的隐私权政策来通知您任何更改。
